A few quick answers
What does "Foundation A Smart Class" mean?
Foundation A is the first Foundation stage. Inside Smart Class, that means all 10 Skills lessons (Cube, Sphere, Cylinder, Box, Cup, Apple, Cone, Ice Cream, Intersecting Forms, Paper Crane) plus the current Creation studio lessons. As we add more Smart Class lessons to the Foundation A scope, you get them too — at no extra cost.
Will future classes be included automatically?
Lessons we add to the Foundation A scope are included in your access. When we open new course families — like Foundation B, or new Creation stations — those will be priced as separate scopes, so you always know what you're buying. We will never silently bundle a new paid scope into your existing access.
Will my saved work and progress carry over?
Yes. Journal entries, badges, and saved cards stay with your account. The free lessons you've already opened remain accessible too.
Can I switch options later?
Yes. Send us a note at hello@feiteamart.com and we'll help adjust your access.
How does the online small group class work?
Groups meet live on Zoom with Ms. Dawn on a scheduled cadence, with a maximum of 4 students per group. Option C also includes the full Smart Class self-study, so you can review and practice between live sessions. After you enroll, we'll be in touch about the next group's schedule.
