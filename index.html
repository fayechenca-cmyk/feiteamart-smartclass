Foundation of Sketch A

An honest skill training in the academic tradition of drawing. Ten lessons — sphere, cube, cylinder, and the basic forms that shape almost everything we draw.

No promises beyond what training can give. Taste, vision, voice — those are yours to find.

"Treat nature by means of the cylinder, the sphere, the cone."
— Cézanne, 1904
This series follows that idea. It's not a method. It's a tool. What you do with it — that's yours.
